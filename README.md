# Revenue-Opportunities-

Revenue Opportunities Dashboard – Power BI Project

Project Overview:
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to identify revenue opportunities across products,
regions, and customer segments. The dashboard provides insights that support data■driven
decision■making and help stakeholders understand performance gaps and high■growth areas.
Objectives:
• Analyze overall revenue performance and sales distribution.
• Identify high■performing and low■performing products, regions, and customer segments.
• Highlight future revenue opportunities by uncovering trends and patterns.
• Provide business users with an interactive and easy■to■use analytical dashboard.
Data Used:
• Sales transactions
• Product details
• Customer segments
• Regional sales data
• Order quantity and dates
Key Metrics (KPIs):
• Total Revenue
• Total Orders
• Average Revenue per Order
• Revenue Growth Trend
• Top and Bottom Performing Products
• Regional Revenue Comparison
Dashboard Visuals:
• Revenue by Region – Bar/Map Chart
• Product■wise Revenue – Column Chart
• Monthly Revenue Trend – Line Chart
• Customer Segment Contribution – Donut Chart
• Top 10 Customers by Revenue – Bar Chart
• Slicers for Product, Region, Customer Segment, and Date
Insights Delivered:
• Identified regions contributing the highest revenue and regions with growth potential.
• Highlighted underperforming products that require better pricing or marketing strategies.
• Revealed seasonal trends in revenue to support promotional planning.
• Identified high■value customers to focus on retention and upselling strategies.
Business Impact:
• Enabled leadership to make informed decisions based on accurate revenue patterns.
• Improved focus on high■opportunity products and customer segments.
• Supported strategic planning by providing clear, interactive insights.
• Enhanced visibility into performance gaps and growth opportunities.
Role & Responsibilities:
• Imported and cleaned data using Power Query.
• Created data model and relationships.
• Designed DAX measures for KPIs and insights.
• Built user■friendly visual layouts following BI best practices.
• Delivered insights and recommendations based on analysis.
Tools & Skills Used:
• Power BI Desktop
• Power Query
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
• Data Modeling
• Visualization & Dashboard Design
• Business Analysis
